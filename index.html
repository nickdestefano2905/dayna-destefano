Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is "energy-first" consulting?

Instead of forcing you to adapt to generic business strategies, I design custom solutions around your natural energy patterns, strengths, and lifestyle. This means you work with your flow instead of against it, leading to sustainable growth without burnout.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients notice improvements in clarity and energy within the first 2-3 weeks. Measurable business results typically appear within 60-90 days as we implement new systems and strategies.

Do you work with specific industries?

I work with service-based businesses, digital products, e-commerce brands, and consultants across various industries. The energy-first framework is universal and adapts to your specific business model.

What's the time commitment for consulting?

Expect 2-4 hours per month for strategy sessions, plus implementation time for the systems we design. The beauty of this approach is that we optimize for efficiency, so you get better results in less time.

Can I switch between consulting packages?

Absolutely. We'll reassess your needs quarterly and adjust your support level as your business grows and evolves.

What if I'm not sure which service is right for me?

Book a free 30-minute discovery call. We'll discuss your goals, challenges, and current situation to determine the best fit for your needs.
